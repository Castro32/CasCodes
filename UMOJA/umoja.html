About The School

Umoja Primary School came into existence in 1985 as a municipal sponsored school to assist the local community gain formal education. The World Bank built a total of 23 classrooms between 1983 and 1984, since there was no school around. In January 1995, the school began officially with learners fetched from neighboring schools that were in existence by then; like Mwijabu, Chaani and Bomu primary school. There were pupils from class 1 â€“ 3 with a population of less than 100 learners. The school has since grown over the years and currently has a total of 1116 learners. The school is located along Changamwe Migadini road, in Kipevu ward, Chaani zone, Changamwe sub county, Mombasa County. The school was registered in 2014 and the registration number is C1S12000003. The school lies on land area of 1.350Ha The first K.C.P.E was done in 1990 with 64 candidates The school participates in other activities apart from academics these include scouts/girl guide clubs, wildlife club, guidance and counseling, drama, athletics and ball games.
